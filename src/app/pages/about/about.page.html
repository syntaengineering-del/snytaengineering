About Us

Building the Future with Engineering Excellence

SynTa Engineering Inc is a full-service MEP engineering consulting firm dedicated to delivering innovative, sustainable, and code-compliant building solutions.

Who We Are

Founded with a vision to transform the built environment, SynTa Engineering Inc has grown into a trusted MEP engineering partner for developers, architects, and building owners across the region. Our team of licensed professional engineers brings decades of combined experience in mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection design.

We specialize in creating high-performance building systems that prioritize occupant comfort, energy efficiency, and long-term operational savings. From concept through construction administration, we are committed to excellence at every phase of the design process.

Our collaborative approach ensures seamless coordination with all project stakeholders, resulting in integrated designs that meet the highest standards of quality and code compliance.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective MEP engineering solutions that enhance building performance, ensure occupant safety, and exceed our clients' expectations through technical expertise and collaborative partnership.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and sought-after MEP engineering firm, recognized for our commitment to design excellence, technological innovation, and our contribution to creating healthier, more efficient, and resilient buildings.
